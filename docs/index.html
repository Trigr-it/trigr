How it works

Up and running
in three steps.

No scripting, no config files, no learning curve. If you can click, you can automate.

1

Pick your modifier

Click Ctrl, Alt, Shift or Win in the modifier bar — or combine them. The keyboard updates to show which keys are already assigned.

2

Click a key

Click any key on the visual keyboard. The action panel opens on the right — choose Type Text, Macro, Send Hotkey, Open App, or more.

3

Use it immediately

Press Assign and your hotkey is live. No restart needed. Press your combo in any app and Trigr fires the action instantly.
